About Me

Ben is a Full Stack Web Developer, Educator, and Musician-Composer based in Philadelphia, PA.
His playing can be heard with various orchestras and ensembles ranging from classical and contemporary, to Pop and Indie groups.

As a recent graduate with a certificate in Full Stack Web Development at the University of Pennsylvania LPS' bootcamp program, Ben looks forward to applying my newly developed skills to solving problems and meaningfully contributing to communities.
